A free nonprofit, built by a student

Not everyone's passion fits in one box.
Find the people who get yours.

PassionPlan connects people chasing less-common interests — like veterinary medicine, falconry, or mycology — with real education plans, nearby resources, and mentors who've already been where you're headed.

No email required. Just a username and a passion.

The problem

Some passions don't come with a community built in.

If you love soccer or coding, it's easy to find a team, a club, a tutorial. But if what lights you up is bird banding, cheesemaking, or exotic-animal rehab, you're often on your own — guessing at what classes to take, where to volunteer, or who to even ask. PassionPlan exists to close that gap, for free.

  • Submit a free short course in your area of experience
  • Every submission is personally reviewed before it goes live
  • Help someone skip the years you spent guessing
How a passion plan works

One path, four stages — like rings closing in on a target.

Every passion on PassionPlan is broken into the same simple journey, whether it's veterinary medicine or bonsai.

STAGE 1

Explore

Learn what the field actually involves day to day, and whether it fits how you like to work.

STAGE 2

Learn

Take the foundational classes and short courses — online or in person — to build real knowledge.

STAGE 3

Practice

Find hands-on places near you — clinics, shops, clubs, or labs — to get real experience.

STAGE 4

Connect

Talk to a mentor or educator who's further along, so you're never figuring it out completely alone.
